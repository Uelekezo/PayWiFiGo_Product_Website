About Us

PayWiFiGo is a Kenyan company that presents a novel approach to boosting connectivity and revenue streams for Kenyan businesses. The enterprise software offers special router software, enabling venues like restaurants and hotels to provide prepaid WiFi access. The groundbreaking solution lets businesses monetize existing infrastrucure, cater to customers' craving for flexible internet usage and offers bundles savings to both customers and businesses.

Here's what they do:

  • Provide a captive portal WiFi system: This system allows businesses to control who has access to their WiFi and how much they pay for it.
  • Offer customizable pricing options: Businesses can set their own prices for WiFi access, depending on the speed, duration, and other factors.
  • Enable secure payment processing: PayWiFiGo uses a secure payment gateway to ensure that businesses get paid for their WiFi services.
  • Bridge the digital divide: PayWiFiGo's mission is to make internet access more affordable and accessible to everyone, especially in underserved communities.
